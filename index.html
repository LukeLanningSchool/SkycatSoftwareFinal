About Skycat Software

Skycat Software is an indie software development team that develop software as a hobby. Projects currently being developed by the team include: video games, computer applications, AI programs, and this website.

Skycat Software was officially founded in 2023 when the team, consisting of three developers, broke off from Starlight Software.

Prior to breaking off, the group (who would eventually form Skycat Software) started working on individual projects, however the only project that was completed and released before separation was Droplets, an arcade-style video game.
